Fernando Sucre is a fictional character from the TV series Prison Break, portrayed by actor Amaury Nolasco. He is Michael Scofield's cellmate at Fox River State Penitentiary and becomes a loyal ally in the escape plan, driven by his desire to reunite with his girlfriend, Maricruz. Sucre is known for his warmth, humor, and unwavering loyalty, providing emotional depth and a contrast to the series' intense drama
