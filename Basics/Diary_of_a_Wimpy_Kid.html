Diary of a Wimpy Kid Rodrick Rules by Jeff Kinney

Jeff Kinney

As Greg starts a new school year, he wants to forget the summer, particularly one event that his older brother, Rodrick, knows about. Greg's secret is at risk of being revealed, especially with a diary involved. Diary of a Wimpy Kid: Rodrick Rules follows Greg as he deals with middle school challenges, tries to impress girls, avoids the school talent show, and attempts to protect his secret.

About Jeff Kinney

Jeffrey Patrick Kinney (born February 19, 1971)[1] is an American author and illustrator. He is best known for creating, writing and illustrating the children's book series Diary of a Wimpy Kid. He also created the child-oriented website Poptropica.
