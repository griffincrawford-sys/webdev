National Football League

The NFL (National Football League) is the highest level of professional American football in the world, featuring 32 teams split into the AFC and NFC conferences, competing in a season from September to January, culminating in the Super Bowl championship game, all centered around a strategy of advancing the ball down the field to score points.

Super Bowl

The Super Bowl, originally known as the AFL NFL World Championship Game, is the annual league championship game of the National Football League of the United States. It has served as the final game of every NFL season since 1966, replacing the NFL Championship Game.
